Gaming

Users will be able to play games in a much different way than traditionally. Using 3D space and the physical world to its advantage, augmented reality gaming offers experiences that involve walking around and surrounding environments to play. Pokémon Go uses light augmented reality to make it seem as if the creatures exist in our physical world.
