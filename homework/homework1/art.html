Art

Traditionally, art is made with a canvas or a screen and somewhat limited by dimensions. With augmented reality, artists can create pieces that take physical surroundings into consideration.1 This may open a new door to modern art and how people view art as well as how they view computers. Apple is aiming to advance this field with the arrival of its ARKit.2
